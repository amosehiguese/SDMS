How to Return an Item

1

Contact Our Support Team

Email us at {{ site_config.contact_email|default:'support@example.com' }} with your order number and reason for return.

Include photos if the item is damaged or defective.

2

Receive Return Authorization

We'll provide you with return instructions and a return authorization number within 24 hours.

This includes the return address and any specific packaging instructions.

3

Pack and Ship the Item

Pack the item securely in its original packaging (if available) and ship it back to us.

We recommend using a trackable shipping method for your protection.

4

Receive Your Refund

Once we receive and inspect your return, we'll process your refund within 5-7 business days.

Refunds are issued to the original payment method.

What Can Be Returned

  • Home goods (unused)
  • Damaged or defective items

What Cannot Be Returned

  • Personal care items
  • Customized products
  • Intimate apparel

  • Worn or used items
