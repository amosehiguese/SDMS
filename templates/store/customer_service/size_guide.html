How to Measure Yourself

Use our size guide to find your perfect fit. All measurements are in centimeters and should be taken while wearing well-fitted undergarments.

Bust/Chest

Measure around the fullest part of your bust/chest, keeping the tape level

Waist

Measure around your natural waistline, at the narrowest part of your torso

Hips

Measure around the fullest part of your hips, keeping the tape level

Sizing Tips & Recommendations

  • If you're between sizes, we recommend sizing up for comfort
  • Check individual product descriptions for specific fit notes
  • Measure yourself without heavy clothing
  • For shoes, measure feet in the evening when they're slightly swollen
  • Consider your preferred fit - loose or fitted
  • When in doubt, contact our customer service for help
